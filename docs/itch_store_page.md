## Description (page body)

> **The machines we trusted turned on us.**
> A 3D first-person shooter where the enemy is an *intelligence* — and it studies you.

Fight back through a **20-level campaign** of rogue drones, androids, siege mechs
and towering bosses — including hazard arenas fought on narrow catwalks over a
**molten sea** and a **flooded reactor** that kill you if you fall in.

### ⚡ The enemy learns you
An **Adaptive AI Director** quietly profiles *how you play* — camp or keep moving,
brawl up close or snipe from range, your headshot rate, which gun you lean on —
and **counters it**:
- Snipe from afar → it sends **SWIFT** rushers to close the gap.
- Out-aim it → it fields **WARDEN** units you can't stagger (so you *dodge*, not suppress).
- Spam one weapon → it rolls out **SHIELDED** armour that shrugs it off.

The overlord even taunts you with what you're actually doing: *"You like your
distance. I'm closing it."* — *"The Shotgun again. I've patched for it."* The read
resets every level, so each sector sizes you up fresh.

### 🔫 A deep arsenal — 17 weapons + 3 grenades
From the starter pistol, SMG, rifle and shotgun up to a heavy/energy tier: a
**Tesla** lightning beam, **Arc-Coil**, **Plasma**, **Nova** scatter, piercing
**Gauss Lance**, **Twin-Rail**, homing **Swarm Launcher**, rocket **Devastator**,
chain-lightning **Tempest Coil**, the **Singularity Cannon**, and the ultimate
**OMEGA-X Annihilator**. Hitscan & projectile, ADS, recoil, charge/volley/slug
alt-fires, piercing/splash/cluster/homing/chain mechanics — plus a frag, an
**EMP**, and a **gravity-well** grenade that herds a whole pack into one knot.

### 🤖 39 enemy types, including bosses
Recon drones, infantry androids, leaping spiders, skitter swarms, suppressing
gunners, charged-beam snipers, kamikaze seekers, shielded brutes, healers, the
leaping **Ravager**, the siege **Warmech**, a **breaching robot shark**, a
**mood-faced war-bot** (cheerful until it locks on), a hovering **buzzsaw drone**
— up to the **Terminator**, sky-dropping **Colossus**, **Titan**, and **Archon**
bosses. Every hostile has a turning-3D **bestiary** entry with its strengths,
weaknesses and counter-weapons.

### 🎬 Built to feel good
Trauma camera shake, dash & slide, dynamic FOV, tracers, muzzle smoke, ejected
casings, surface-aware impacts, bullet-hole & scorch decals, enemy
flinch/stagger/last-stand and death topples — wrapped in themed per-level lighting
& fog, cinematic post, **adaptive combat music** that swells in a fight, comic-book
story cutscenes, an interactive campaign map, an **Armory** upgrade shop, a Gun
Range, a Horde survival mode, and a built-in level editor.

Keyboard + mouse **and** full gamepad support. Four graphics tiers
(Low → Ultra). EASY / NORMAL / HARD with checkpoints.

*Made with Godot 4. Nearly everything — geometry, enemies, weapons, FX and all
audio — is generated in code.*
